Welcome to INTERPOL

INTERPOL is an international organization that facilitates worldwide police cooperation and crime control. We connect police forces across the globe to make the world a safer place.

Our mission is to prevent and fight crime through enhanced international cooperation.

About Us

Founded in 1923, INTERPOL has 195 member countries and operates 24/7 to support law enforcement agencies worldwide.

We provide secure communication channels, databases of criminal information, and operational support for investigations.
